#Russian Troll Tweets Analyzing Tool
www.fivethirtyeight.com published 3 million tweets from Russian trolls:


https://fivethirtyeight.com/features/why-were-sharing-3-million-russian-troll-tweets/


The tweets are available as a series of csv (comma-separated value) files stored on a public Github repository: https://github.com/fivethirtyeight/russian-troll-tweets

The codes should answer the following questions:
What were the dates of the 10 days that contained the most tweets?
What hour of the day had the most tweets?
What is the most common word that occurs across these tweets?
What are 10 most common words used by each author?
